The Story

Why A Letter of Hope?

Born from a personal journey through cancer, one letter at a time.

When words feel small, a letter can hold them.

After my own cancer diagnosis, I learned something surprising: the smallest gestures carried the most weight. A handwritten note from a friend arrived on a day I couldn't get out of bed. I kept it beside me, re-reading it on hard days.

That single letter sparked something. I started writing to others in treatment, and to friends navigating depression and loss. Every time, the response was the same — "This meant more than you know."

A Letter of Hope was born to do just that: put real, tangible love into envelopes and send it to people who need reminding that they are not alone.

Every letter is written by hand, from my heart to theirs.

— With hope, always
