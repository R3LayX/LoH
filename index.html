The Story

Why A Letter of Hope?

Born from a personal journey through cancer, one letter at a time.

A Letter of Hope was born during one of the hardest seasons of my life. After being diagnosed with stage 3 cervical cancer in 2024, I experienced firsthand how heavy difficult days can feel—and how deeply a kind word, a thoughtful gesture, or simply knowing someone cares can matter. Through treatment, I saw others facing those same long days, sometimes alone, and it stayed with me. I created A Letter of Hope to send comfort, encouragement, and a little light through the mail—because no one should have to feel like they are walking through a hard season alone.

Every letter is written by hand, from my heart to theirs.

— With hope, always
