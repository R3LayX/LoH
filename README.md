# Letter of Hope ✉️

> Sending encouragement one stamp at a time

## About

Letter of Hope (LoH) is a community-driven initiative dedicated to spreading positivity and encouragement through handwritten letters and thoughtful correspondence. In a digital world, we believe in the power of personal, tangible messages to uplift spirits and build human connections.

## Mission

To inspire and encourage others by delivering meaningful letters that remind people they are valued, supported, and never alone in their journey.

## How It Works

- Write letters of encouragement, support, or inspiration
- Share your message with our community
- Help deliver hope to those who need it most

## Features

- 📝 Easy letter submission and sharing
- 💌 Community-driven encouragement
- 🌍 Connect with others through thoughtful correspondence
- ✨ Make a positive impact, one letter at a time
